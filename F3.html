Gliss

Year
2025
Type
Freelance
Scope
Visual Art Direction | Brand identity

Gliss is a software company that delivers lean, clean, and innovative cloud-native solutions. My role was to develop the visual identity for this newly founded brand, including the logo, color palette, mockups, social media templates, and company presentation deck slides.

The biggest challenge was undoubtedly the logo design. The name Gliss is derived from glissando—a musical term describing the smooth transition between notes, much like the seamless and efficient software solutions the company provides. The key challenge was to translate this concept into a tangible, tech-inspired, and minimalist visual identity that effectively communicates the brand’s essence.
